Our Goal

STSM is a system that is able to predict single-trial subsequent memory. Predictions will include for both STM and LTM: 1) whether or not the subject will recognize the word 2) the subjective experience (remember/ know); and 3) his level of confidence. The Prediction will be based on single-trial EEG activity during the study phase.
